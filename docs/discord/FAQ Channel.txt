__**DOWNLOAD SYSTEM**__
```css
[Pixiv doesn't work.]
Make sure you are logged in or reset the login.
[Tumblr doesn't work.]
Try turning on the GDPR option under: Network>Downloaders>DEBUG>do tumblr GDPR click-through.
[How do i download from SadPanda?]
Import the preset and copy over your cookies from your browser!
[How do i import tags from a website?]
You can set client wide default tag import options under: Network>Downloaders>Manage Tag Import Options.
Or per download tab under tag import options individually.
[Where do i find new Download Presets?]
At the preset github: https://github.com/CuddleBear92/Hydrus-Presets-and-Scripts
[How do i import a Preset .png image file?]
Import under Network>Downloaders>Import Downloaders
[How do i RIP/Download a whole site?]
Generate the urls outside of the client (Notepad++) and import it into the url downloader or gallery downloader tabs.
[How do i access restricted content?]
Login on the Client, add your login details in the login script or import the Cookies from your browser.
```
